About

Strategic guidance for sport

Fondamenta Consulting is a management consultancy company providing strategic and advisory services to the sports industry, with a particular focus on football. Drawing on over a decade of professional experience in the sector, the company supports organisations with strategic guidance, industry expertise, and advisory consulting.

The name Fondamenta derives from the Venetian term for the canal-side walkways of Venice — a place of perspective, vision, and new opportunities.

Founder

About the founder

Julia Nasi

Founder & Principal Consultant

Julia founded Fondamenta Consulting with roots in two cultures — Italian and Portuguese — and an international outlook shaped by years of work across Europe and beyond. With over a decade of professional experience in the sports industry, she brings informed, direct, and relationship-driven counsel to clients navigating complex environments.

The company's name reflects a personal connection to Venice, where the fondamenta — the walkways that line the city's canals — represent both place and purpose: a foundation on which to build, and a vantage point from which to see clearly.
